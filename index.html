The Perfect French Getaway

Take time out and explore the wonders of the Dordogne and Perigord Noir

Les Fousseaux is a charming old farmhouse, nestled in the peaceful French countryside on the boarder between the Dordogne and Perigord Noir.

Brimming with character, the original stone house was contructed more than 200 years ago alongside the old road into Chavagnac. As time went by the main house was expanded, and an assorment of farm buildings constructed, including a forge, a barn and pig stys.

Many features have been lovingly restored and adapted so that, while it is now used as a place of peace and relaxation, the house still retains the hallmarks of its working past.
